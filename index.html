Sorter for gallery entries. Pick your tournament source(s), and hit the Start button.

Click on the entry you like better from the two shown,

or tie them if you like them equally.

Sort only the 3rd tournament for voting purposes please.


Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) J/DownArrow (undo) K/UpArrow (tie) L/RightArrow (pick right) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).

1/2/3 always correspond to the top/middle/bottom vertical buttons.
